Biography

Bin Liao received his B. Eng degree in 2006 and M. Eng degree in 2009, both in Electronic Engineering, from Xidian University, and received his Ph.D  degree from The University of Hong Kong in 2013. He was a research assistant at The University of Hong Kong. He is currently a full Professor at the College of Information Engineering, Shenzhen University, Shenzhen, China.
